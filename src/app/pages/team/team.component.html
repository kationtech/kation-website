Andoni Albert
President and CEO,
Kation Technologies

Andoni Albert is a Filipino business leader and serial entrepreneur. His ventures have aimed to bring Filipino IT talent to the world stage by engaging businesses of every scale, from multinationals to SMEs.

Andoni was among the first students of BS Management Information Systems in Ateneo de Manila University, where he graduated college in 1999, and MS Information Technology and Computer Science in 2001. Since then he has built a number of IT businesses, including Quark Data, PeopleSoft, 24/7 International, and, most recently, Kation Technologies.

As President and CEO of Kation, Andoni is focusing on leveraging emerging technologies for SMEs, putting new technology in the hands of frontliners, service professionals, and educators. With Kation, Andoni seeks to elevate workers and communities everywhere.
