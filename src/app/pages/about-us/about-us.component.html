Who we are

Founded in 2015, Kation Technologies is an IT solutions company in the Philippines serving clients who seek to digitally transform their businesses. Kation Technologies is a Microsoft Gold Partner which began as the largest Filipino Certified Microsoft Dynamics AX Partner, and is now a pioneer in implementing and creating solutions for all three Microsoft Clouds on the Microsoft Dynamics 365 M365 and Azure platforms.

What we believe in

Kation is derived from the word “cation,” which means positively charged ions. At Kation Technologies, we believe in the power of creating positively charged connections. We believe in allowing our customers to make better decisions and bringing efficiency to make their business grow.
