Who we are

Founded in 2015, Kation Technologies is an IT solutions company in the Philippines serving clients who seek to digitally transform their businesses. Kation Technologies is a Microsoft Gold Partner which began as the largest Filipino Certified Microsoft Dynamics AX Partner, and is now a pioneer in implementing and creating solutions for all three Microsoft Clouds on the Microsoft Dynamics 365 M365 and Azure platforms.

How we work

As your trusted partner, our goal is to bring you peace of mind by empowering your business to be operationally excellent and make KPI monitoring easier. We can help you level up your workforce productivity using human-centered design, tools, and systems on platforms that best suit your needs.

Why work with us

When you work with us, expect a high degree of responsiveness, reliability, and collaboration. We seek long-term partnerships with clients across industries, whether it be in the areas of manufacturing, retail, distribution, hospitality or education. We bring the tools, the people, and the commitment necessary to help you achieve the maximum potential of your business.

Andoni Albert
is a Filipino business leader and serial entrepreneur. His ventures have aimed to bring Filipino IT talent to the world stage by engaging businesses of every scale, from multinationals to SMEs.

Andoni was among the first students of BS Management Information Systems in Ateneo de Manila University, where he graduated college in 1999, and MS Information Technology and Computer Science in 2001. Since then he has built a number of IT businesses, including Quark Data, PeopleSoft, 24/7 International, and, most recently, Kation Technologies.

As President and CEO of Kation, Andoni is focusing on leveraging emerging technologies for SMEs, putting new technology in the hands of frontliners, service professionals, and educators. With Kation, Andoni seeks to elevate workers and communities everywhere.
